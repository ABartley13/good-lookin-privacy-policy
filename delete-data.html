BLACC Data Deletion Requests

Last updated: March 22, 2026

This page explains how BLACC users can request deletion of their account and associated personal data.

Request deletion by email:

Send your request to info@weareblacc.com with the subject line: BLACC Data Deletion Request.

What to Include in Your Request

  • The email address used for your BLACC account.
  • Your display name (if known).
  • A clear statement that you want your account and data deleted.

What We Delete

  • Account access data related to your BLACC login.
  • Profile information stored with your account.
  • User-generated content connected to your account where feasible within system architecture.

What May Be Retained

We may retain limited information where required for legal, security, fraud prevention, dispute handling, or policy enforcement purposes.

Verification and Timeline

  • We may ask you to verify account ownership before processing deletion.
  • Verified requests are typically processed within 30 days.

Related Policy

For broader privacy details, see the BLACC Privacy Policy.

If you submitted your request and need a status update, reply to your original request email so we can track your case faster.
