Privacy Policy for BLACC

Last updated: March 22, 2026

BLACC ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and share information when you use the BLACC mobile application and related services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account and authentication data (such as email address and user ID).
  • Profile information (such as display name, discipline, bio, social links, profile photo, and public contact email if provided).
  • User content (such as forum threads/replies, resource/event submissions, and uploaded media/files).
  • In-app activity and notification history required for product features.

2. How We Use Information

  • Authenticate users and provide account access.
  • Enable profile, forum, resource, and event functionality.
  • Deliver in-app reminders and notifications.
  • Support moderation, safety, abuse prevention, and service reliability.

3. Data Storage and Providers

BLACC uses Firebase services, including Firebase Authentication, Cloud Firestore, and Firebase Storage, to operate core features.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Information may be shared only with required service providers, when legally required, or to protect users and platform integrity.

5. Data Retention

We retain information as needed to operate BLACC, maintain integrity, and satisfy legal obligations. On valid deletion requests, we take reasonable steps to delete or anonymize data unless retention is legally required.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may request access, correction, deletion, or restriction of your personal information.

7. Children

BLACC is not directed to children under 13 (or higher age where required by local law).

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The current version and date will be posted in the app or associated channels.
